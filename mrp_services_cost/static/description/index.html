MRP Services Cost

Bring service expenses into your manufacturing cost

Track, allocate, and post service costs directly on Manufacturing Orders. Service lines are pulled from BOM service components and their cost is added to the finished product valuation.

Key Features

  • Service lines on Manufacturing Orders with quantity, unit cost, and subtotal.
  • Auto-load service lines from BOM service components.
  • Total service cost computed and stored on the MO.
  • Creates a valuation layer for service costs at completion.
  • Posts accounting entries for real-time valuation products.
  • Supports overhead or expense accounts and analytic accounts per line.

Configuration

  • Set an overhead account in Settings > Manufacturing.
  • Define service products with appropriate cost and accounts.
  • Use service components in BOMs to populate MO service lines.

Compatibility

Odoo 18.0 Community and Enterprise.
